# Employee Policies & FAQs

## 1. Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to act professionally and ethically at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind will not be tolerated.

## 2. Information Security Policy
Employees must protect company data and systems from unauthorized access.
Sharing internal documents or credentials with external parties is strictly prohibited.

## 3. Acceptable Use of Technology
Company devices and systems are to be used primarily for business purposes.
Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with work responsibilities.

## 4. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Remote employees must ensure secure internet connections and protect confidential information.

## 5. Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue PTO on a biweekly basis.
PTO requests must be submitted through the HR system and approved by a manager.

## 6. Expense Reimbursement
Business-related expenses must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence.
Receipts are required for all expenses over $25.

## 7. Who do I contact with policy questions?
Contact the People Operations team at people-ops@company.com.
